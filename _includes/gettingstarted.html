Getting Started with FEDSIM

FEDSIM's assisted acquisition focuses on complex acquisition requirements that can range from IT to professional services. Our acquisition and program management staff has a deep understanding that allow us to delivery high quality solutions. FEDSIM follows the FAR/DFAR and agency specific acquisition regulations.

1

Interagency Agreement and Waivers

FEDSIM’s authority for Interagency Agreements is derived from:

  • USC 11302(e) (OMB’s executive agent designation) and/or the Acquisition Services Fund, 40 SC § 321.
  • USC § 501-502 (for personal property and non-personal services).
  • USC § 11314 (for some aspects of technology).
These statutory authorities are independent of theE Economy Act and therefore, the Economy Act does not apply.

2

Funding Documentation/MIPR

FEDSIM’s assisted acquisition fee is determined based on the unique requirements of the acquisition and will be included in the funding document/ Military Interdepartmental Purchase Request (MIPR).

We accept and manage multi-agency funding.

3

Introduction to the IPT

FEDSIM acquisitions are managed by Integrated Project Teams (IPTs), and one will be assigned to you.

The IPT works with clients to ensure everything runs smoothly, meets client requirements, and it compliant.

A FEDSIM Acquisition Manager (AM) and Project Manager (PM) will work with the requesting agency to write the Statement of Work (SOW) as part of our service.
