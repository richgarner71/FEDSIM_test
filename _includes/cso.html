Commercial Solutions Opening (CSO) Pilot Program

The General Services Administration (GSA) established a pilot program to competitively procure innovative commercial items, technologies and services using the Commercial Solutions Opening (CSO) porcedure. To learn more, visit the Procurment Innovation Resource Center (PIRC) website.

Interested in learning more?

Government Clients

Our FEDSIM tean assists clients with many types of acquisitions and will work with you to determine the best acquisition strategy to fulfill your requirements. Reach out to us at fedsim@gsa.gov to see how we can help.

Industry Partners

You can respond to compete on an opportunity by submitting a brief about your solution. If there is a good match between your solution and our client's requirements, you will be asked to provide a full proposal.
