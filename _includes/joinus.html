Acquisition

Contracting Specialists (CS) and Contracting Officers (CO) leverage their knowledge of complex acquisition policy to pinpoint contractually compliant solutions that solve each client's unique challenges. They are solution brokers, leaders, and quality managers.



Project Manager

Project managers and Acquisition Project Managers plan, organize, and execute client projects from start to finish to ensure that industry partners deliver high-quality solutions on time and on budget. They are client advocates, ensuring client engagenment and satisfaction throughout the project life cycle.
