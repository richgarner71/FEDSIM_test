Committees
The FIAC has four active committees If you interested in joining, please complete the interest form online.

Streamlined Acquisition

Focuses on improving the procurement process and overal FEDSIM experience, whether through policy, best practices, or process change.

Small Business

Focuses on engaging the small business community, whether through GSA, AAS, FEDSIM opportunities, matchmaking with large businesses, or as outreach to the federal GOVCON small business community.

COVID-19 Working Group

Focuses on process and policy impacts resulting from the global coronavirus pandemic as it relates to GSA AAS operations. Outputs from this group inform the larger streamlines acquisition committee.

Marketing/
Outreach

Focuses on educating the federal market on GSA AAS FEDSIM, sharing successes, promoting compitition, and fostering awareness of FEDSIM's capabilities.
